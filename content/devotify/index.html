Cinderella

branding, illustration, interactive, motion

As an Interactive Designer at Situation Interactive, I designed landing pages, e-mail, banners, social assets, as well as storyboarded and animated the "It’s Possible" lyric video for CINDERELLA.

I enjoyed working on CINDERELLA since I was able to enhance the branding by incorporating other fonts and designing simple graphics to give it more of a fairytale quality. I especially enjoyed working on the “It’s Possible” lyric video and photo cards. At the time I was working on the video (in the Summer of 2013), there was a trend toward making interactive content and lyric videos for Broadway shows. I like kinetic typography and it was a great opportunity to experiment with it in After Effects.
