NextEMR

NextEMR was a revolutionary Electronic Medical Record application, perhaps a decade ahead of its time. It was one of the first web-based EMRs to be CCHIT certified, and the first with a focus on UX. It was also the first to utilize a fully-integrated load balancing task system.

The mission for NextEMR was to be easy to use, improve efficiency, transparency, accountability, and overall patient care of practices.

In addition to all the usual features required by the CCHIT, notable features included:

  • Cross-platform cloud-based system without the need for expensive hardware
  • An integrated smart load-balancing task and notification system that kept track of follow ups, regularly or custom-scheduled patient care (ie, a once-a-month blood test) depending on diagnoses, appointment scheduling, approvals, prescription refills, misc tasks, and more. Tasks were assigned to employees based on role and workload.
  • Patient follow up/scheduling recommendation engine based on diagnoses
  • Integration with billing and pharmacy software
  • Patient access to all of their medical records with login credentials

Technology

HTML/CSS/Javascript/jQuery with AJAX, driven by a modular MVC PHP architecture.
