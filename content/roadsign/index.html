Road Sign Visit Website

Road Sign is an electronic proof of delivery application for Android and iOS that allows instant access to pick-up, inspection and delivery information by replacing paper forms used by vehicle transporters.. This eco-friendly solution gives the transporter the ability to conduct an accurate inspection of the vehicle(s), including direct photo integration, at both pick-up and delivery. These inspections details can be viewed throughout the lifecycle of the transport, eliminating the uncertainty often associated with when vehicle damage may have occurred.

Road Sign allows for electronic signature capture, and the ability to email or fax the signed BOL as required by the client. A lifecycle history is available for review on the app, providing a summary of all events associated with the vehicle(s).

Features include:

  • Cutting‐edge technology delivers a superior customer experience
  • Increase efficiency of the pickup and delivery process
  • Get 24/7 real time status updates and tracking on your vehicles
  • Real time data eliminates faxing or printing delays along the way
  • Immediate insurance claim documentation with instant upload of vehicle images
  • Eliminates paper meeting industry standards and “Be Green” initiative

Road Sign is currently used to deliver millions of vehicles per year. MetroGistics was recently acquired is recognized as a leader in automotive logistics.

Technology

  • The app is a cross-platform (iOS and Android) hybrid (HTML5/Javascript/Obj-C/Java) application with Cordova, running a custom view-component Javascript framework built with HMJS.

    Performance, modularity, and cross-browser compatibility were the main considerations. The apps were designed to "feel native," despite being written with HTML5.
  • PHP REST API, built with a lightweight, modular custom HMVC framework.
